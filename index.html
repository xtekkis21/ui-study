Monitor Panel Types: From IPS to the future of OLED

You are currently viewing this page in Light Mode.

Nowadays, monitors use different panel technologies which have their distinct strengths. IPS (In-Plane Switching) panels have accurate colours and wide viewing angles making them a popular pick for creative work. VA (Vertical Alignment) panels are known for their deeper blacks and higher contrast ratios which is ideal for movie watching and gaming. However, both of these technologies struggle to compete with OLED, which lights up each pixel individually. This way OLED achieves perfect blacks, near-instant response times and even better colour accuracy than IPS. OLED is widely considered the best panel technology available today. Moving forward, Mini-LED appears to be a strong competitor, offering improved brightness and contrast over traditional LED panels at a lower price than OLED, making it a promising option for the mainstream market.
